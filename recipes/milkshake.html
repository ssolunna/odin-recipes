Chocolate Banana Milkshake

Description

Yummy milkshake for those hot summer days!

Ingredients

  • 1 banana, frozen and chunked
  • 6 tablespoons powdered chocolate-flavored malt drink mix
  • 1 cup milk
  • 2 cups vanilla ice cream

Steps

  1. Place the frozen banana chunks, powdered drink mix, milk, and vanilla ice cream into a blender.
  2. Blend until smooth and creamy.
  3. Pour into large glasses.
